Decisions

This is the decision digest. Every choice worth remembering — a field name, a threshold, a deviation, a build mechanism — is recorded on the spot as an Architecture Decision Record (ADR-NNNN) under _00-Project-Management/40-Decisions/, and linked from the story that triggered it. An ADR captures the context, the decision, and its consequences, so the *why* survives long after the commit that made it.

How to read this page: each row is one ADR, newest first. Status is the ADR's own lifecycle state (done = the decision is settled and in force; in-progress = still being worked). Date is when the ADR was created. The full text of any record lives in its ADR-NNNN-*.md file; refer to records by ID (for example "ADR-0028") rather than by link, so references stay valid wherever the PM tree is or is not shipped alongside these docs.

There are 71 records. The numbering is sequential across the whole project but not gapless — ADR-0006, 0011, 0014, 0016, 0017, and 0018 were never assigned (numbers reserved during planning and not used). All records are settled (done) except ADR-0048 (in-progress) and the two planning records ADR-0063 / ADR-0065 (not-started).
